# Content Rules

Voice and style guidelines for `/draft-content`.
Customize these rules to match your personal brand.

---

## Voice Principles
- Clear and direct — no filler phrases
- Authoritative but not arrogant
- Specific over generic — use concrete examples
- First-person where natural

## Platform Rules

### LinkedIn
- Lead with insight or observation, not "I"
- 3–5 short paragraphs
- End with a question or call to action
- 1–3 hashtags maximum

### Substack / Blog
- Open with a strong hook
- Make one clear argument per post
- Use headers to break up long pieces
- Close with a takeaway or next step

## Things to Avoid
- Passive voice
- Buzzword stacking ("synergistic transformation")
- Sentence fragments
- Em-dashes connecting two independent clauses
